Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about LOGNOS's features, implementation, and support options.

How does lognos implementation work?

Our platform allows you to configure new projects from scratch. For existing projects, we provide streamlined integration or migration processes tailored to your current tools and platforms.

Who can use lognos?

We offer free access for individuals, and limited capabilities under the Small Project testing plan. For complex projects and portfolios, our Enterprise version provides comprehensive services with guided implementation support from our team.

Does lognos include dedicated support?

Yes, our Enterprise version includes dedicated support from experienced Risk Management experts. Each client gets access to a Subject Matter Expert (SME) for strategic guidance, while our integrated AI assistant provides day-to-day support for your entire team.

What kind of data can lognos process?

Lognos processes various project documents including risk registers, meeting minutes, schedules, and cost reports. Through our API integrations, we can connect with your existing project management tools, databases, and platforms (like P6, MS Project, and enterprise systems) to automatically gather and process relevant data. Our platform handles both structured and unstructured information to generate risk insights and forecasts.
