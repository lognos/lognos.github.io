Lognos tracks project risk evolution over time, analyzing how mitigation actions affect your risk profile as they are implemented. The platform automates data collection from emails, meeting minutes, and team communications, streamlining risk management beyond traditional workshops while reducing workload for risk managers.

Drawing from past project knowledge, Lognos identifies successful mitigation strategies and applies them to current scenarios. The system provides data-driven recommendations based on team input and historical patterns, enabling regular risk profile updates. Each risk is assigned to specific owners with planned timelines and forecast completion dates.

With lognos Agent, teams focus on interpreting data rather than collecting it. The platform captures and preprocesses information automatically from various communication channels, creating a comprehensive knowledge base that informs current risk assessments and improves project outcomes.
